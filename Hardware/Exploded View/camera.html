    • Lens array

    The L16's lens array, a combination of 28mm, 70mm, and 150mm modules, is responsible for shifting the camera's mirrors when the photographer is trying to zoom. Whenever a shot is fired, these lenses work with their respective mirrors and sensors to capture 10+ images, which are later fused together.
